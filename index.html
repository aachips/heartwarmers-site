About

This website aims to prevent freezing deaths this winter. It offers resources for helpers, businesses, and people at risk. The long-term goal is to develop an app connecting those at risk with essential resources.

How it Works

Heartwarmers is a project that collects and distributes reusable bottles with hot liquids to those in need during the winter months. It's a collaborative effort between local businesses, non-profits, and churches to provide warmth and comfort to vulnerable individuals. The project is open source and not-for-profit.

Three levels of Engagement

Level 1: Individual

We'll show you how to create and personalize care packages for those in need, even on a budget. We'll also connect you with resources for bulk item donations.

Level 2: Business

Businesses and nonprofits can use our toolkit to provide essential amenities like warmth, charging stations, and food to those in need. Organizations with free public offerings can also add themselves to our interactive map.
